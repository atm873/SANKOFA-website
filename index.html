Mission

To empower individuals and communities through holistic support services and transformative skills development initiatives that promote self-reliance, dignity, and long-term impact.

Vision

A thriving, inclusive society where all individuals—regardless of background—have the opportunity to realize their full potential through access to support, education, and skills.

Core Values

  • Empowerment: Building the capacity of individuals and communities.
  • Inclusivity: Embracing diversity and ensuring equal access to opportunities.
  • Integrity: Operating with honesty, transparency, and accountability.
  • Compassion: Serving with empathy and care.
  • Collaboration: Working together for sustainable change.
  • Innovation: Seeking creative and practical solutions for evolving needs.

Strategic Objectives

  1. Deliver Holistic Support Services: Provide psychosocial, educational, and material support that addresses comprehensive needs.
  2. Implement Skills Development Initiatives: Offer accessible training programs for practical, marketable skills.
  3. Promote Socio-Economic Inclusion: Facilitate pathways for employment, entrepreneurship, and further education.
  4. Foster Community Participation and Leadership: Encourage active involvement in decision-making and leadership roles.
  5. Build Strong Partnerships: Collaborate to expand impact and sustainability.
  6. Monitor, Evaluate, and Improve: Continuously assess and improve programs.

Organizational Summary

We provide a wide range of services—including Employee Assistance Programs (EAP), expert consulting, legal and auditing services, and comprehensive school counselling and aftercare. Our Skills Development Centre is at the heart of what we do.

The Centre addresses social challenges such as crime, substance abuse, and unemployment, equipping individuals with practical skills for employment and entrepreneurship.
